I'm excited to announce the launch of this personal brand site. Built with Docusaurus, it serves as a central hub for my work across four content sections.

## What's Here

### Research
Long-form studies, analyses, and deep explorations of topics that matter.

### Featured
Appearances on other people's platforms — podcast episodes, conference talks, interviews, guest posts, and more.

### Insights
Blog-style posts with ideas, thoughts, and notes on technology, design, and building things.

### Newsroom
Announcements, milestones, and updates on projects and work.

## Technical Details

The site is built with:

- **Docusaurus v3** with TypeScript
- **Multiple blog instances** — three independent blogs with separate RSS feeds and tag systems
- **Local search** via `@easyops-cn/docusaurus-search-local`
- **Analytics** via Plausible (privacy-friendly, no cookies)

## RSS Feeds
