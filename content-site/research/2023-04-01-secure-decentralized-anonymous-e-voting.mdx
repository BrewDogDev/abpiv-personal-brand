## Abstract

This paper presents a secure and decentralized anonymous e-voting scheme that addresses the challenges of privacy, transparency, and trust in electronic voting systems. The proposed scheme leverages cryptographic techniques to ensure voter anonymity while maintaining verifiability and preventing double-voting.
